Techkshetra is a technical as well as non-technical event organised by the students of Polytechnic,
The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda. Techkshetra comprises of two words, one is Tech
which means Technology and the other word is Kshetra which meams field, ultimately,
the meaning of Techkshetra is the field of technology, where students get an opportunity
to exhibit their technical as well as non-technical skills.
Techkshetra'17 was organised from 16th to 18th February of 2017, with a participation of
over 1200 students from entire Vadodara as well as from other parts of Gujarat including
Ahmadabad, Surat, Rajkot, Bhavnagar,Bharuch, Godhra etc.
